    what i stand for

    STEM education in schools

    Growing up, my school did not offer courses geared toward students who were interested in STEM, apart from the standard math and science ones. I was exposed to engineering and computer science through the Johns Hopkins CTY summer programs, however, not everyone has the opportunity to attend such programs. I believe that STEM courses are just as important as any other traditional course and that all students should have access to it.
